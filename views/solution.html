What makes HomeBase different?

Forge real connections

Forget about filters and having to craft the perfect post. Your life is meant to be shared with the people who matter most. HomeBase makes it easy to strengthen relationships with family no matter how far you're spread apart.

Keep tabs on all the fam

HomeBase makes it easy for the whole family to stay in the loop -- even grandma! It's brining extended families into one place, so you can stay connected beyond the Christmas get-together and occasional reunion. It's the feed you actually should be scrolling!

Like a Family Tree, but cooler and living

Never stumble into a family reunion blind ever again wondering who Uncle Bob's kids are. HomeBase is the perfect solution for extended families to see how everyone is linked together and make sure no one is forgotten.
